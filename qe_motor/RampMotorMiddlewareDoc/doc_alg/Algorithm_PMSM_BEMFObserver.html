BEMF Observer

Overview

The BEMF Observer provides the ability to estimate the back EMF from the voltage and current readings of the motor.

BEMF Observer

The BEMF observer estimates the back EMF from the measured voltage and current based on the motor’s voltage equation. The voltage equation on the dq axis is expressed as follows:

v_d = R*i_d + L_d*di_d/dt - ω*L_q*i_q
v_q = R*i_q + L_q*di_q/dt + ω*L_d*i_d + ω*λ

where λ is the magnetic flux of the rotor, and ω is the electrical angular velocity. The BEMF is calculated as follows:

e_d = -ω*L_q*i_q
e_q = ω*L_d*i_d + ω*λ

PLL-based angle and velocity estimation

PLLs are used to estimate the angle and velocity of the rotor from the estimated BEMF. The phase error is calculated as follows:

θ_err = atan2(e_q, e_d) - θ_est

Input this phase error into the PI controller to estimate the speed:

ω_est = Kp*θ_err + Ki∫θ_err dt
